Configurable Weekdays

You can select your organization's configurable days of week for filter out weekends at the time of add organization. You also change this settings.

Manage Holidays

Holidays added by admin are yearly listed for all other users of organization. Holidays added by admin are yearly listed for all other users of an organization.

Manage Leaves

HR can simply approve or reject the leaves applied by the employees. The system will instantly notify the employees of the actions performed by the HR.

Manage missing punch

Employees can apply the missing punch request if they forgot the punch. HR of admin can accept or reject the request. The list of missing punch shows the status of the request.

Geofencing Support Available

This feature allows an admin to set geographical areas to limit where an employee is permitted to mark attendance.

External System Integration

An organization can have a key that is used for machine integration. The employees also get the unique machine-ID per organization.
